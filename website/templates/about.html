About Bioworld

An MMORPG where biotechnology meets economic strategy. Learn, Do, Teach.

Vision

Bioworld is a highly dynamic, collaborative, and competitive MMORPG where scientific discovery is directly linked to massive economic and infrastructural development. Powered by AI and distributed systems technology, players solve real biotechnology challenges while building corporations and monetizing their discoveries.

The Core Model: Learn, Do, Teach

The game follows a proven pattern that allows players to monetize their scientific insights into scalable offerings:

  • Learn - Master AI tools, understand protein structures, study genomics
  • Do - Predict protein folds, design therapeutics, run simulations
  • Teach - Create courses, expose APIs, build educational content

Phase 1: Biotechnology Loop

The initial gameplay centers around foundational biotechnology challenges using real scientific data:

  • Protein Folding - Use AlphaFold-inspired AI to predict 3D structures from amino acid sequences
  • Generative Design - Create novel proteins using RF Diffusion-like techniques for antibodies, enzymes, and structural proteins
  • Wet Lab Validation - Validate AI predictions with simulated laboratory experiments
  • Multi-Omics Data - Integrate genomics, proteomics, and clinical data for personalized medicine

Phase 2: Corporation & Economy

The "Grand Theft" style emerges through strategic deployment and monetization of discoveries:

  • Player Corporations - Build biotech empires with research teams, manufacturing, and financial systems
  • API Marketplace - Expose your models as APIs. Charge for drug efficacy predictions and genetic analysis
  • Teaching Platform - Create courses and guides. Monetize expertise through educational offers
  • Market Trading - Trade proteins, patents, and assets using reinforcement learning strategies

Distributed System Design

The entire MMORPG functions as a distributed system where components collaborate, communicate, and compete by sending messages over a network:

  • Scalability - Partitioning divides workload across nodes, thinking globally while acting locally
  • Reliability - Replication ensures data availability in presence of failures
  • Consensus - Distributed consensus for coordinated state management across the game world
  • Holarchy - Systems composed of subsystems, from atomic labs to major corporations

AI Applications in Curing Disease

The game leverages transformative applications of AI in medicine and biology:

  • AlphaFold-inspired Prediction - Determine protein structures from sequences
  • Drug Discovery - AI-accelerated identification of drug candidates
  • Personalized Medicine - Model interactions between proteins, DNA, RNA, and drug molecules
  • Misfolding Analysis - Understand how genetic mutations cause protein misfolding
  • NLP for Research - Extract insights from medical records and research publications

Economic Systems

  • Reinforcement Learning - Develop algorithms for market prediction strategies
  • Game Theory - Strategic decision-making for resource allocation and competition
  • Auctions & Market Design - Trade discoveries, patents, and research assets
  • Ethical Considerations - Navigate patents, bias, fairness, and transparency
